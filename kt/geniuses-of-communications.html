A.1 Alexander Graham Bell

Figure A.1: Alexander Graham Bell (Source: Moffett Studio - Library and Archives Canada / C-017335, Public Domain, Link)

  • Life time: 1847 – 1922
  • Nationality: English and American
  • Profession: Engineer
  • Contribution: Invention of the telephone

A.2 Abbé Claude Chappe

Figure A.2: Abbé Claude Chappe (Source: H. Rousseau - Album du Centenaire; photographs by User:Havang(nl), Gemeinfrei, Link)

  • Life time: 1763 – 1805
  • Nationality: French
  • Profession: Engineer
  • Contribution: Optical telegraphy (see Appendix B.1)

A.3 Johann Carl Friedrich Gauss

Figure A.3: Carl Fiedrich Gauss (Source: Christian Albrecht Jensen - http://archiv.bbaw.de/archiv/archivbestaende/abteilung-sammlungen/gesamtbestand-des-kunstbesitzes/gelehrtengemaelde/gelehrtengemalde-seiten/ZIMM-0001.html, Public Domain, Link)

  • Life time: 1777 – 1855
  • Nationality: German
  • Profession: Mathematician
  • Contributions:
    • Gaussian distribution
    • Telegraphy
    • and many, many more

A.4 Heinrich Rudolf Hertz

Figure A.4: Heinrich Rudolf Hertz (Source: Robert Krewaldt - http://wellcomeimages.org/indexplus/imageM0014750.html, Public Domain, Link)

  • Life time: 1857 – 1894
  • Nationality: German
  • Profession: Physicist
  • Contributions:
    • Experimental proof of electromagnetic wave propagation
    • Photoelectric effect

A.5 Robert Hooke

Figure A.5: Robert Hooke (Source: Rita Greer - The original is an oil painting on board by Rita Greer, history painter, 2004, FAL, Link)

  • Life time: 1635 – 1703
  • Nationality: English
  • Profession: Natural Philosopher
  • Contributions:
    • Hooke’s law
    • Microscopy

A.6 Guglielmo Marconi

Figure A.6: Guglielmo Marconi (Source: Pach Brothers - This image is available from the United States Library of Congress’s Prints and Photographs division under the digital ID cph.3a40043, Public Domain, Link)

  • Life time: 1874 – 1937
  • Nationality: Italian
  • Profession: Engineer
  • Contribution: First wireless long-distance radio transmission (see Appendix B.3)

A.7 James Clerk Maxwell

Figure A.7: James Clerk Maxwell (Source: George J. Stodart - Frontpiece in James Maxwell, The Scientific Papers of James Clerk Maxwell. Ed: W. D. Niven. New York: Dover, 1890., Public Domain, Link)

  • Life time: 1831 – 1879
  • Nationality: Scottish
  • Profession: Theoretical Physicist
  • Contributions:
    • Displacement current
    • Maxwell’s equations
    • Prediction of electromagnetic wave propagation

A.8 Alec Harley Reeves

  • Life time: 1902 – 1971
  • Nationality: English
  • Profession: Engineer
  • Contribution: Pulsce-Code-Modulation (PCM)

A.9 Claude Elwood Shannon

Figure A.8: Claude Elwood Shannon (Source: Konrad Jacobs - https://opc.mfo.de/detail?photo_id=3807, Public Domain, Link)

  • Life time: 1916 – 2001
  • Nationality: US American
  • Profession: Mathematician and Engineer
  • Contribution: Mathematical Theory of Communications

A.10 Wilhelm Eduard Weber

Figure A.9: Wilhelm Eduard Weber (Source: Rudolph Hoffmann, Public Domain, Link)

  • Life time: 1804 – 1891
  • Nationality: German
  • Profession: Physicist
  • Contributions:
    • First use of \(c\) for speed of light
    • Work on magnetism
    • Telegraphy
